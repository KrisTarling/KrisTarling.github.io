I made my first website in 1998, when I was 10. Unsurprisingly, it wasn't very good. It was a fansite for Goldeneye on the N64 and it was everything you imagine when you think of websites from the 90's. It had a black background. It had unnecessary animated flame GIFs. And, of course, it featured the ubiquitous 'Under Construction' sign. Today, we expect so much more from the web. And, as a developer, I strive to create intuitive yet remarkable experiences. But, above all, I still try to hold onto that enthusiasm for the primeval web. When content always came first, because we had little else to play with...
